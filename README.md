# visitax-guide
## VISITAX Mexico Guide

The **VISITAX** is a tourist tax required for international visitors leaving the Mexican state of Quintana Roo, including Cancun, Playa del Carmen, Tulum, and the Riviera Maya.

Travelers are required to pay this tax before departing the region. The process can be completed online.

## What is the VISITAX?

The VISITAX is a government tourism tax introduced to support tourism infrastructure and services in Quintana Roo.

## Who must pay the VISITAX?

International visitors leaving Cancun, Playa del Carmen, Cozumel, and other destinations in Quintana Roo.

## How to pay the VISITAX

Travelers can pay the **visitax** online before their departure.
